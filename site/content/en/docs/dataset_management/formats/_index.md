In CVAT, you have the option to export data in various formats.
The choice of export format depends on the type of annotation as
well as the intended future use of the dataset.

The table below outlines the available formats for data export in CVAT.

| [Cityscapes 1.0](format-cityscapes)                                                                                         | .txt<br>.png  | Semantic<br>Segmentation                                    | U-Net, SegNet, DeepLab, <br>PSPNet, FCN, ERFNet, <br>ICNet, Mask R-CNN, HRNet, <br>ENet, and others.                                                                                    | Polygons, Masks                                                                                 | Specific attributes  | Not supported |
| [COCO 1.0](format-coco)                                                                                                     | .json         | Detection, Semantic <br>Segmentation                        | YOLO (You Only Look Once), <br>Faster R-CNN, Mask R-CNN, SSD (Single Shot MultiBox Detector), <br> RetinaNet, EfficientDet, UNet, <br>DeepLabv3+, CenterNet, Cascade R-CNN, and others. | Bounding Boxes, Oriented Bounding Boxes, Polygons, Masks                                                                | All attributes       | Supported     |
